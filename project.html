Welcome to WordFairly!

Your Favorite Word Guessing Game

This interactive and engaging game challenges you to guess a randomly selected word. Here's how to play:


Start the Game:
    Click the "Play" button.
    Click the "Submit Guess" button to make a guess.
    Enter a word of the same length as the secret word.
Hints:
    The game provides hints after each guess.
    Correctly guessed letters are revealed in the hint.
    Letters that occur at the same position in the secret word are uppercase in the hint.
    Incorrect letters are represented by underscores.
Guess Limit:
    You have a maximum of 10 guesses to reveal the secret word.
Winning:
    If you correctly guess the entire word within the limit, you win!
    The game displays the number of guesses and the time spent.
Losing:
    If you reach the guess limit without guessing the word, the game ends.
    The correct word is revealed
Start a New Game:
    To start a new game, click the "Restart" button or refresh the page.

Enjoy the challenge of deciphering the secret word and test your word-guessing skills!
